The HistoryOf Our Tech Hub

Sky Tech Hub was established by a group of youngsters known as CODESTARS from Ndalani city in the year 2019. This is after they all graduated from different universities and succeded with high master of programming. Its just after they did some projects together,developed websites and made some systems that they came up with the idea of how programmers will come together in a certain hall and share ideas on to improve the technology. The idea of building the Hub was borrowed from the Ihub which is located at CBD Nairobi Town.
